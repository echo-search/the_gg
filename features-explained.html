The Girl Gang — Explore the Features

Dive in and discover all the playful tricks and surprises on this page. Each feature comes with a “Try it” challenge — just follow along!

Hover Effects

Card pulse & glow

Move your mouse over a person card (like "Jaya") to see it gently pulse and glow. Move the mouse away to stop it.

✨ Hover over a card and watch it shine like Jaya in the spotlight!

Floating ring (desktop only): On devices that support hover, a tiny ring follows your mouse inside the card. It disappears when you leave.

Pointer & Click Visuals

Cursor trail

Move your mouse and see tiny glowing dots follow your path, fading like magic.

💫 Move your mouse quickly and leave a sparkling trail behind!

Tap glow

Click or tap anywhere to see a short-lived glow at that spot.

🌟 Click or tap and make your mark like a true Girl Gang member!

Ripple effect

Click or tap to watch a ripple appear from where you touched, fading out gracefully.

💧 Tap anywhere and watch the ripple dance across the page.

Card Motion

Tilt effect

Move your pointer inside a card to tilt it in 3D. It goes back to normal when you move the pointer away.

🌀 Slide your mouse across a card and feel the tilt in action!

Mobile title glow: Moving your phone or tablet can make the page title glow with excitement.

Click to Reveal

Bonus quotes

Click a card to reveal hidden quotes. Click again to hide them.

💌 Click a card and uncover secret Girl Gang quotes!

Confetti & Celebration

After the page loads, confetti flies around for a little celebration. You can also launch it manually.

🎉 Reload the page and join the confetti party!

Background & Decorations

  • Floating particles drift across the page.
